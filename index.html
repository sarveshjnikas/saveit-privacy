Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

SaveIt is a personal bookmarking app for iOS. This policy explains what data SaveIt does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

None. SaveIt stores all your saved items — links, notes, images, and categories — exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, and we do not operate any backend.

Data Shared with Third Parties

SaveIt does not share any data with third parties. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting SDKs, and no advertising frameworks.

In-App Purchases

SaveIt offers an optional one-time premium upgrade processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not see or store any payment information. Apple's purchase privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Permissions

SaveIt's Share Extension may request access to images or content you explicitly choose to share from other apps. This content is saved locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

SaveIt does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
